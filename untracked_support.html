Frequently Asked Questions

How does Untracked detect workouts?

The app reads heart rate data from Apple Health and identifies periods where your heart rate was significantly elevated above your personal resting baseline. You can review these periods and log them as workouts — even if you forgot to press "start."

Can I adjust how workouts are detected?

Yes. Open Settings (gear icon) to change the detection sensitivity and minimum workout duration. "More" sensitivity picks up lighter activity, "Less" only flags intense sessions. The minimum duration controls how long an activity must last to be detected (5–60 minutes).

What are Workout Reminders?

An optional feature that checks for untracked workouts once a day at a time you choose. If any are found, you get a notification. Tap it to open the app with the detected workout ready to save. Enable it in Settings — it uses minimal battery.

What data does the app access?

Untracked reads heart rate samples, workouts, body measurements, distance, and step count from Apple Health. It writes workouts and associated data (calories, distance) back when you save. All processing happens on your device — nothing is sent anywhere.

Why don't I see any heart rate data?

Make sure you've granted Untracked permission to read Health data. Go to Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Untracked and enable heart rate reading. You also need a device that records heart rate to Apple Health.

Will logged workouts appear in Apple Health?

Yes. When you save a workout in Untracked, it's written to Apple Health and appears in the Fitness app alongside your other workouts.

Does the app work without an Apple Watch?

Untracked works with any device that records heart rate to Apple Health — Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, Oura, chest straps, or any compatible wearable.

What do the colored dots on the calendar mean?

The calendar shows dots on days where untracked workouts were detected. The color ranges from green (few) to red (many), giving you a heatmap of your unlogged activity.

What languages are supported?

English, Ukrainian, Spanish, German, French, Japanese, Portuguese (Brazil), Italian, and Polish. The app follows your device language automatically.
